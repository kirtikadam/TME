Trekking, one of the adventure getting very much popular in India these days. As India is one of the country with full of adventurous places like Forts, Nature landscapes, Lakes, Forests, Wildlife Sanctuaries, Beautiful hilly regions, mountains, flower valley, Seashore, and many more. Sahyadri mountain range is full of trekking locations and forts built by "The Great Maratha King Warrior Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaja", are situated in the western Maharashtra. Trekking destinations in Maharashtra are one of the best outdoors recreational activity to do, Sahyadri Mountain ranges of Maharashtra has many excellent tourist destination and best monsoons getaways around Mumbai and Pune. Pune has pleasant and very romantic climate throughout the year, which makes it one of the green city, surrounded by rolling hills and lush green forest along with number of forts and trekking places. KONKAN is a coastal strip of land bounded by the Sahyadri hills on the east and the Arabian Sea on the west. Maharashtra's Konkan area consists of Raigad, Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg and Thane districts. How To Participate

Participation is open to :
All educational institutions and trekking clubs.
Individual members in the age group of 14 to 50 (age limit relaxable for those who are physically fit). Past experience is not a must for the treks, but practice of walking / trekking for 5-10 kms daily, is advisable.
Application forms should be completely filled by the participant himself/herself in capital letters. It should be submitted along-with two latest photographs (passport size), a physical fitness certificate from a registered M.B.B.S. doctor, along-with Cash.
